Lean engagement

My engagement model is meant to be easy for you.

  • 1 week iterations - frequent checkpoints allow us to learn and adjust quickly.
  • No long-term obligations - we plan sprint-to-sprint without contracts, which makes it easy for you to start and reduces your risk.
  • Pay for what you need - engage week-to-week, press pause whenever you like, and scale up or down my contribution effort (see below).
  • Get what you pay for - receive assets for the project with each iteration that you own and possess.

Costs

I charge for data science contribution units, which are priced at $1000 per unit. Each contribution is equal to approximately 1/4 the effort of a full-time experienced Data Scientist.

In planning, I estimate how many units each discrete body of work would entail in a logarithmic scale—0.1, 0.2, 0.5, 1, 2, 5 units—based on complexity. Planning is not a commitment on your part—you decide priorities and select what work I actually take on. Then, you pay for the units that we agree upon for a task, not the actual work time.

Get started

  1. Email me to tell me a little bit about your project and some of your goals or objectives.
